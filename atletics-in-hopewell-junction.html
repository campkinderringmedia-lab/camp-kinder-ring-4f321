Frequently Asked Questions About Atletics

What types of athletic programs does Camp Kinder Ring offer for children in Hopewell Junction, NY?
Camp Kinder Ring provides a wide range of athletic services for campers, including soccer, basketball, swimming, tennis, baseball, and gymnastics. The camp is designed to accommodate various age groups and skill levels, ensuring that every child can participate and develop their athletic abilities in a fun, supportive environment.
How much does it cost to enroll my child in the summer athletics program at Camp Kinder Ring?
The pricing for Camp Kinder Ring’s summer athletics program varies based on session length and the specific activities your child enrolls in. For Summer 2024, the standard four-week session is $5,200, which includes all athletic programming, meals, and equipment usage. Discounts are available for siblings and early registration. Please contact our Hopewell Junction office for details on custom sessions or financial assistance.
Are your coaches and instructors certified or licensed to teach athletics?
Yes, all of our athletic coaches and instructors hold relevant certifications in their specific sports, such as USA Swimming certification for our lifeguards and coaches, and National Youth Sports Coaches Association credentials for team sports. Additionally, all staff are trained in CPR and first aid, and undergo thorough background checks in compliance with New York State camp regulations.
What does the enrollment and orientation process look like for new campers interested in athletics?
After registering online or in person at our Hopewell Junction campus, each new camper is invited to a pre-camp orientation, where we assess skill levels and discuss interests to tailor the athletic experience. Parents will complete required health forms and waivers, and campers will be fitted for any necessary equipment. Our staff will review safety protocols and answer questions before the camp session begins.
What happens if my child is unhappy with the athletics program or wants to switch activities? Are there any guarantees?
We want every camper to have a positive experience. If your child is not satisfied with their assigned athletic program, we allow transfers to other available sports within the first week, subject to space and skill assessments. While we do not offer full refunds after the session has started, we work closely with families to address concerns and ensure your child finds a program they enjoy.
